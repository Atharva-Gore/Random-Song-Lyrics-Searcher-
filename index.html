Random Song Lyrics Searcher

Type an artist and click Search — the app will try multiple songs until it finds lyrics.

—

How it works

  1. Search an artist → app fetches songs (iTunes API).
  2. It requests lyrics for random tracks (lyrics.ovh).
  3. When lyrics are found, a random line is chosen and shown as a quote card.
